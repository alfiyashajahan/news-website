U.S. pushes for more defence deals, offers F-35 fighter jet

February 15, 2025 01:57 am IST - New Delhi

Starting this year, we’ll be increasing military sales to India by many billions of dollars, says U.S. President Donald Trump; the countries announce they will review their respective arms transfer regulations, including International Traffic in Arms Regulations, or ITAR; new initiatives such as TRUST announced, as leaders pledge to accelerate defence technology cooperation across Space, air defence, missile, maritime and undersea domains.The U.S. will be increasing military sales to India “by many billions of dollars” starting this year, President Donald Trump said on Thursday at the joint press conference with Prime Minister Narendra Modi, while also offering America’s most advanced military platform, the F-35 fifth generation fighter jet, to India.However, Foreign Secretary Vikram Misri later pointed out that there was a process to be followed for military procurements and that the formal process in this regard hadn’t started “as yet”.
